## Questions of Interest
Given that the most important thing in a data science project is *the question*, let's jot them down.
* Did the attic air sealing project in 2019 reduce our energy consumption?
  * Can trend analysis identify when in the timeline we did the work?
* Can we predict next month's energy bill based on historical data?
  * What does a simple regression tell us?
  * What variables should we include in the model?
  * What other data could we wrangle to improve the prediction?
